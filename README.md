## Ecommerce Platforms

### Tools and Platforms

- [Shopify](https://www.shopify.com/) - Hosted ecommerce platform with themes, apps, and built-in payments. Free trial available.
- [WooCommerce](https://woocommerce.com/) - Free WordPress plugin that turns any site into a full online store.
- [OpenCart](https://www.opencart.com/) - Free open source PHP shopping cart with a large extension marketplace.
- [PrestaShop](https://www.prestashop.com/) - Free open source ecommerce platform with 300+ built-in features.
- [Medusa](https://medusajs.com/) - Open source headless commerce engine built with Node.js.
- [Saleor](https://saleor.io/) - GraphQL-first open source ecommerce platform built with Python and Django.
- [Vendure](https://www.vendure.io/) - Open source headless commerce framework built with TypeScript and NestJS.
- [WordPress](https://wordpress.org/) - Free CMS that powers WooCommerce and dozens of other ecommerce plugins.
- [Cloudflare Pages](https://pages.cloudflare.com/) - Free static site hosting, useful for headless storefronts.

## Selling Online

### Tools and Platforms

- [Amazon Seller Central](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/) - Free to sign up, sell on the largest marketplace in the world.
- [Etsy](https://www.etsy.com/sell) - Marketplace for handmade, vintage, and unique goods. Free to open a shop.
- [eBay Seller Hub](https://www.ebay.com/sh/landing) - Free selling dashboard with analytics, listing tools, and order management.
- [Printful](https://www.printful.com/) - Print on demand fulfillment with no upfront costs. Integrates with Shopify, Etsy, and more.

- [Gumroad](https://gumroad.com/) - Sell digital products directly to your audience. Free to start.
- [Payhip](https://payhip.com/) - Sell digital downloads, courses, and memberships with a free plan.
- [Google Merchant Center](https://merchants.google.com/) - Free product listings in Google Shopping search results.
