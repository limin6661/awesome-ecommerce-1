# Contributing to Awesome Ecommerce

Thanks for your interest in contributing.

## Guidelines

- Search existing entries before adding a new one to avoid duplicates.
- Each entry should be a genuinely useful, free (or free tier) tool or resource.
- Use the format: `[Name](URL) - Brief description of what it does.`
- Keep descriptions short and factual.
- Add new entries at the bottom of the relevant section.
- One pull request per addition or change.

## Quality Standards

- Tools must be actively maintained and functional.
- Resources must be free or have a meaningful free tier.
- No affiliate links.
- No paid-only tools unless they are the clear industry standard with a free trial.

## How to Submit

1. Fork this repository.
2. Add your entry to the appropriate section in README.md.
3. Submit a pull request with a brief explanation of why the resource belongs here.
